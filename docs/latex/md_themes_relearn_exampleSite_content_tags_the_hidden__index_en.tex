This lists all pages that are hidden from the home page. This includes pages that define {\ttfamily hidden=true} in their frontmatter as well as descendents of hidden pages.

When giving term pages, you should give it a title. Otherwise it will print out a warning and will use the urlized title, which may looks weird.
